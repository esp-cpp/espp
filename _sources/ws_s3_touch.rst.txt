Waveshare ESP32-S3 TouchLCD
***************************

WS-S3-Touch
-----------

The Waveshare S3 TouchLCD is a development board featuring the ESP32-S3 module.
It includes a small 1.69-inch touchscreen display, a buzzer, a 6-axis IMU, and a
real-time clock (RTC).

The `espp::WsS3Touch` component provides a singleton hardware abstraction for
initializing the touch, display, IMU, and audio subsystems.
